Angle 45 White Water Rafting

Get ready to trade the daily grind for the roar of the river. Our whitewater expeditions take you deep into the heart of untamed rapids, where every splash is a surge of pure adrenaline. Guided by world-class experts, you’ll navigate legendary Class III to V drops, test your limits, and experience the ultimate rush of teamwork in the wild. It’s more than a trip; it’s a heartbeat-pounding journey through nature’s most powerful forces

History

Founded on the principles of precision and adventure, Angle 45 takes its name from the ideal entry angle for a raft to "punch" through a wave without stalling. We launched following the recent resurgence in outdoor interest, building on a history that saw rafting transition from a survival necessity into a global sport featured in the Olympics. While early outfitters often prioritized grit over comfort, Angle 45 utilizes the latest self-bailing rafts and high-strength Hypalon materials to ensure every trip is as safe as it is exhilarating.
